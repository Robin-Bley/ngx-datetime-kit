i18n & Date Formats

Shows three different date formats (DE, US, ISO) and how labels can be overridden per component.

German (dd.MM.yyyy)

formats: NGX_DATE_TIME_FORMATS_DE

US (MM/dd/yyyy)

Global providers can be overridden with component-specific providers. Here via providers: [{{ '{' }} provide: NGX_DATE_TIME_FORMATS, useValue: NGX_DATE_TIME_FORMATS_US {{ '}' }}].

formats: NGX_DATE_TIME_FORMATS_US

ISO 8601 (yyyy-MM-dd)

formats: NGX_DATE_TIME_FORMATS_ISO
